Workers' list

Monitor all of your workers in a single list where you can filter workers by different criteria, order them by live data, and take different actions in bulk or per particular worker. Workersâ€™ list unites all of your GPU rigs and ASIC machines.

Worker's profile

You can examine each worker in details by visiting its profile. Besides system information, you will also see historical data, hardware analysis, different diagnostics, events, and you will be able to take actions for that specific worker.

Control room

In control room you can organize your workers to fit your actual establishment. With this, you will be able to create a heat map and get a quick overview of your whole mining operation in one screen.

Address editor

Address editor allows you to save all of your favorite pools and your wallet addresses under different tags. Tags can then be used in configurations, profit switch, and scheduler.

Worker config

You can choose between various different mining clients, coins, and pools, depending on the system and type of your hardware. Additional config values, such as temperature limits, number of GPUs or boards in the machine, or electricity costs will help you customize each of you workers.

Config templates

Advanced configuration can be saved as a config template so you will always have it within your reach. Config templates can be loaded directly from workers' list or worker's profile and also to the profit switching custom configurations.
